# Solar Panel Troubleshooting
## Panel Not Charging
1. Measure voltage at panel terminals with multimeter (should be 18-22V in sun)
2. Check fuse in charge controller - replace if blown
3. Inspect MC4 connectors for corrosion - clean with dry cloth
4. Check charge controller display for error codes
5. Verify battery is not fully charged already

## No Voltage from Panel
1. Check for shade or dirt on panel surface - clean with water
2. Inspect cables for damage or loose connections
3. Test each panel separately if you have multiple
4. Check bypass diodes if one panel in series is bad

## Common Fixes
- Dirty panel: wipe with damp cloth
- Loose MC4 connector: press until it clicks
- Blown fuse: replace with same amperage
- No soldering needed for most fixes
