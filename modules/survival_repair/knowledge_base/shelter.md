# Emergency Shelter Guide
## Van/RV Shelter Improvements
1. Insulate floor with foam mats - ground cold is biggest heat loss
2. Hang blanket between cab and living area - smaller space to heat
3. Cover all windows at night - reflective insulation works best
4. Ventilate slightly to prevent condensation buildup

## Emergency Shelter Outside Vehicle
1. Tarp lean-to - string tarp between trees at angle, stake far edge
2. Snow shelter - dig into snowbank, make fist-sized vent hole
3. Debris hut - pile leaves and branches over stick frame
4. Always insulate from ground - more heat lost to ground than air

## Choosing Camp Location
1. Avoid valleys - cold air sinks, fog collects
2. Avoid ridgelines - exposed to wind
3. Choose slightly elevated flat ground with natural windbreak
4. Check for widow-makers - dead branches above camp
5. Stay 60m from water - insects, flooding risk, regulations

## Condensation Control in Van
1. Cook with lid on pots
2. Crack window 1-2cm when sleeping
3. Use moisture-absorbing crystals
4. Wipe surfaces morning with dry cloth
