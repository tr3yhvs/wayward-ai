# Emergency First Aid
## Altitude Sickness
1. Descend immediately if severe
2. Rest, hydrate, no alcohol
3. Ibuprofen for headache
4. Descend 500m if no improvement

## Heat Stroke
1. Move to shade immediately
2. Cool with wet cloth on neck/armpits
3. Fan continuously
4. Hydrate with water + salt

## Wound Care
1. Stop bleeding with direct pressure
2. Clean with clean water
3. Apply antibiotic if available
4. Cover with clean bandage
5. Watch for infection signs
