> ⚠️ DISCLAIMER: Always purify water before drinking regardless of source. Contaminated water can cause serious illness or death.

# Advanced Water Guide
## Finding Water in Desert
1. Dry riverbeds - dig 30cm in outer bend where water table is highest
2. Animal trails converging downhill lead to water
3. Green vegetation in dry landscape = water below surface
4. Morning condensation - wipe plants with cloth, wring out

## Finding Water in Mountains
1. Follow valley downhill - water collects at lowest point
2. Snowmelt streams safe if no human activity above
3. Springs from rock faces - usually clean, test with purification anyway
4. Rain collection - use tarp funnel into container

## Emergency Water from Vehicle
1. Radiator water - NOT safe to drink, contains antifreeze
2. Windshield washer fluid - NOT safe, toxic
3. AC condensation - safe to drink if collected clean
4. Canned food liquid - safe and contains electrolytes

## Signs of Water Contamination
1. Unusual smell or color - avoid
2. Dead animals nearby - serious contamination
3. No aquatic life in stream - chemical contamination
4. Oily film on surface - petroleum contamination
Always purify: boil 3 min, or iodine tablets, or filter + UV
