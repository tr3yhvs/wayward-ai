> ⚠️ DISCLAIMER: Always purify water before drinking. Contaminated water can cause serious illness. When in doubt, do not drink.

# Water Sourcing Guide
## Finding Water
1. Follow animal tracks downhill
2. Look for green vegetation in dry areas
3. Collect morning dew with cloth
4. Follow birds at dawn/dusk

## Purification
1. Boiling: 1 min at sea level, 3 min above 2000m
2. Iodine tablets: 2 tabs per liter, wait 30 min
3. Filter: use cloth + charcoal + sand layers
4. Solar disinfection: clear bottle in sun 6+ hours
