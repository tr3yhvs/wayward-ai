# Tire Repair Guide
## Flat Tire
1. Park on flat ground, apply handbrake
2. Loosen lug nuts before jacking
3. Jack up vehicle at correct jack point
4. Remove flat, mount spare
5. Tighten lug nuts in star pattern
6. Lower vehicle, fully tighten nuts

## Tire Plug Kit
1. Locate puncture with soapy water
2. Ream hole with rasp tool
3. Insert plug with insertion tool
4. Trim excess plug
5. Inflate to correct pressure
